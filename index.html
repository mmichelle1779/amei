OUR APPROACH

Amei Atelier approaches fragrance as a daily ritual — designed to be worn close to the body, unfold slowly, and support emotional balance throughout the day.

Drawing from Traditional Chinese Medicine, French perfumery, and Western aromatherapy, our fragrances are composed with restraint, intention, and care for sensitive senses.

HOW IT FEELS TO WEAR

Amei Atelier fragrances sit close to the skin, with soft projection and no sharp edges.

They unfold slowly, becoming warmer and quieter over time rather than louder.

Often, the scent fades before you notice — but the sense of calm remains.

We work with limited palettes, lower concentrations, and a deliberate approach to formulation.

We avoid overpowering notes, aggressive projection, and unnecessary complexity.

Every choice is made in service of comfort, ease, and longevity on the skin.
